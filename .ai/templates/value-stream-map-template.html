❌ Approval sits in queue; no priority signal; batch delays (2-3 day wait)

❌ Info might be incomplete; Finance re-keys data (duplicate entry, risk of errors)
Finance → Bank/Reimbursement
❌ Batch processing; multiple days of wait; employee has no visibility

Top Pain Points & Bottlenecks

🚧 Bottleneck #1: Manager Approval Queue
Managers batch-approve expenses once per week. If you submit Friday, you wait until next Thursday. This is the biggest delay.
🚧 Bottleneck #2: Finance Data Re-entry
Finance re-keys the same data that Employee entered. Duplicate work, error-prone. Every month, errors require corrections.
🚧 Bottleneck #3: Email as Workflow System
Entire workflow runs on email. Emails get lost, buried, forgotten. No audit trail. No priority signal. High friction.

Bottleneck Resolution

Current Bottleneck
Manager batch approval (2-3 day wait) is the biggest constraint. Everything downstream waits for this.
How Product Solves It
Real-time notifications + mobile one-tap approval eliminates the batch queue entirely. Manager approves instantly from anywhere.
Downstream Impact
With manager approval instant, Finance receives approvals immediately. They can process same-day. Combined with automated data flow, reimbursement happens in hours, not days.
