### Raw Notes

**Q1 - Current Workflow**:
```
[Paste or transcribe their description of how they currently solve this]

Example: "We get receipts via email, I manually enter them into QuickBooks,
then email to my manager for approval, then she approves and forwards to accounting."
```

**Q2 - Biggest Pain Point**:
```
[What frustrated them most?]

Example: "The biggest pain is the approval bottleneck. My manager approves once a week in batch,
so if I submit on Friday, I don't get approval until the following Thursday."
```

**Q3 - Impact**:
```
[Time, errors, downstream effects?]

Example: "Means employees wait 7-10 days to get reimbursed. Some of them had to cover
the expense from their own pocket. I heard from one that it created financial stress."
```

**Q4 - Workarounds**:
```
[How do they currently cope?]

Example: "I sometimes ask the accountant to process it out of turn if it's urgent, but that's
extra work for her. Or I remind employees manually when it's coming due."
```

**Q5 - Must-Have Outcomes**:
```
[What would change if we fixed this?]

Example: "If we could get approvals same-day, employees would get reimbursed within 48 hours.
That's the difference between them covering expenses from their personal cash vs. company reimbursing."
```

**Q6 - Constraints**:
```
[Tools, compliance, tech requirements?]

Example: "We're on QuickBooks Online, so anything we use has to integrate with it.
Also, we have to maintain an audit trail for accounting and tax purposes."
```

**Q7 - Usage Patterns**:
```
[Mobile/desktop? When/where?]

Example: "Employees travel a lot for client work, so they need mobile. They'd probably
submit during the client visit when they have the receipt in hand."
```

**Workflow Walkthrough**:
```
[If you watched them do it, capture what you actually observed]

Example: "I watched Sarah open Gmail, search for receipts, copy amounts into a Google Sheet,
then forward to her manager. It took her 8 minutes per expense. She made a typo that I caught."
```

**Q8 - Would They Use It?**:
```
[Interest level? Hesitation?]

Example: "Absolutely. She said if it saved her 30 minutes a week and employees got faster
reimbursement, she'd use it in a heartbeat."
```

**Q9 - Who Else?**:
```
[Other personas to interview?]

Example: "She recommended I talk to the Finance Director about policy constraints,
and to an employee about what they'd need for mobile adoption."
```
