## Product Vision

Status Reporting becomes the standard tool that knowledge workers in product and delivery roles use to surface their contributions automatically — so that career visibility is determined by the quality of their work, not the quality of their self-promotion.

---

## Mission Statement

We automatically aggregate and synthesize weekly activity from the tools knowledge workers already use, so they can deliver a professional, manager-ready status report in under 10 minutes.

---

## Strategic Filters

Every Roadmap item, Release Plan, and Feature Document will be tested against these three filters:

1. **If it requires the user to manually input data, we don't build it.**
   The value proposition is zero-effort capture. Anything that adds input friction to the user's week is off-strategy and must be deferred or rejected.

2. **If the output could embarrass the user with their manager, we don't ship it.**
   Professional quality and review-before-send are non-negotiable floors. Features that bypass the user's editorial control or degrade output quality fail this filter.

3. **If it expands beyond the single-manager audience (in v1), we defer it.**
   Multi-audience distribution, team reporting, and dashboards are future scope. Scope creep into team-level visibility is explicitly out until the single-user, single-manager use case is proven.

---

## Why Now

Frontier GenAI models (OpenAI, Gemini, M365 Copilot) have reached a quality threshold where cross-source synthesis of professional narrative prose is feasible at practical cost. The APIs required (Microsoft Graph, Atlassian REST, Bitbucket REST) are mature and available. The problem has existed for years — the technology to solve it credibly has only arrived now.

---

## Success Looks Like (in 3–5 years)

Knowledge workers in product and delivery roles no longer spend cognitive energy on self-promotion logistics. Their contributions are automatically surfaced, their managers have accurate visibility into their work, and their career outcomes reflect the quality of what they actually do — not how well they remembered to document it on Friday afternoon. Status Reporting is the invisible layer that makes this possible across the M365 and Atlassian ecosystem.
